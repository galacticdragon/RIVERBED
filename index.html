Welcome to Riverbed

Riverbed is a complete software package for businesses specilizing in "revolving-door" rental operations. Just like a river, relative to you, the user, there is an upstream side and a downstream side.
Riverbed uses this methodology to streamline the rental process from new order all the through to when the items come back through the door. Riverbed breaks the operation down into upstream and downstream modules, which can be assigned to different members of your team.
The upstream module focuses on customer relations as well as ingesting orders. The downstream module handles back-office operations such as inventory, shipping/receiving, accounting, support, and fulfillment.
